Why love running with us?

Feel Tired

Wake up early or end your workday on a sweaty note. Be late to dinner with your family. Participate in diet culture by appeasing health trolls.

Feel Trapped

Phyiscial exercise is a hobby. But unlike the your fun hobbies that your friends make fun of, your friends will make fun of you for not running.

Get Social

Make friends with people who aren't your friends and who are secretly judging not only your pace, but your shoes. Also, they're juding your body.

Feel Strong

Feel strong by giving into peer pressure. Facebook says do it, Instagram says do it, Dr Oz says do it, and all your insufferable friends say do it.
